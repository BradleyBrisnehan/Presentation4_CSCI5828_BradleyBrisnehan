Trial Download

In an effort to expand the ease of access and adoption of Visual Studio for new process development, a trial/free download of each version is provided by Microsoft. A free download of Visual Studio Community is offered, provided you are an individual developer working on your own free/paid applications, a member of an organization providing training or contributing to open source projects, or up to five members as part of a non-enterprise organization. A trial download of Visual Studio Enterprise is provided and can be upgraded to a paid package at a later date. Both downloads as well as the functionality supported are listed here.

Once you have chosen the free/trial download best suited for your application, you can begin developing mobile as well as desktop applications. Rather than try to reiterate content published across the internet, a recommended starting place is here. Hosted by MSDN (Microsoft Developers Network), this site is a collection of projects from contributors as well as Microsoft themselves. Pick the project which most interests you and begin playing.
